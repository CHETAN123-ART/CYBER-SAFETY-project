Cyber Fraud Awareness

Think Before You Click • Stay Safe Online

Introduction

Cyber fraud is any crime carried out using computers, mobile phones or the internet to steal money, personal information or digital accounts. Criminals use fake websites, calls, emails and social media to trick people. Knowing these scams helps us stay safe online.

1. Phishing

What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cyber attack where criminals send fake emails, messages or websites that look genuine to steal passwords, bank details or OTPs.

Example:

"You've won ₹50,000! Click here to claim."

✔ Never click unknown links.
✔ Verify the sender before sharing information.

2. Vishing

What is Vishing?

Vishing (Voice Phishing) is when scammers call pretending to be bank officers, police or customer care to obtain confidential information.

Example:

"Your bank account will be blocked. Tell me your OTP."

✔ Banks never ask for OTPs or PINs over phone calls.

3. Identity Theft

What is Identity Theft?

Identity theft happens when someone steals your personal information like Aadhaar, PAN, passwords or photos and uses them without permission.

✔ Use strong passwords.
✔ Enable Two-Factor Authentication.

4. Online Banking Fraud

What is Online Banking Fraud?

Fraudsters gain unauthorized access to bank accounts using fake websites, malware or stolen passwords and transfer money illegally.

✔ Check bank statements regularly.
✔ Never share banking credentials.

5. Social Media Scam

What is a Social Media Scam?

Scammers create fake profiles, giveaways or impersonate friends to steal money or personal information.

✔ Accept friend requests only from people you know.
✔ Verify suspicious messages.

Safety Tips

  • Never share OTPs or passwords.
  • Use strong passwords.
  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication.
  • Keep software updated.
  • Avoid suspicious links.
  • Report cyber crimes immediately.
